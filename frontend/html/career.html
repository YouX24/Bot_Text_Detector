We're at the tipping point of an AI revolution

The world is witnessing a transformative era driven by artificial intelligence (AI). At the forefront of this revolution, we strive to harness the potential of AI to create innovative solutions for text analysis and understanding.

Our Mission: Preserve What's Human

Our mission is to utilize AI to distinguish genuine human expression in text, preserving the essence and authenticity of human interaction amidst the rise of AI-generated content. We believe in the importance of genuine human connection and aim to protect it in this digital age.

How it Began

The journey started with a passion for technology and a vision to bridge the gap between human expression and AI. The founders, driven by the belief in the significance of authentic human interaction, embarked on a mission to develop a tool that can distinguish between bot-generated and human-generated text, ultimately striving to preserve what's human.

Our Team
